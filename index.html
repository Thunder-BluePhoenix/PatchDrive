How to Play

The Loop

Drive your machine as far as possible. Disasters strike periodically — survive them for coins. Parts fail over time; when one breaks, draw a replacement in the panel to install it.

Drawing

Follow the guide shape when drawing. Circles for wheels, long tubes for pipes, flat shapes for wings, triangles for thrusters, blocks for armor. Better shape = higher quality = more HP and coins.

Progression

Earn bank coins each run. Spend them on upgrades between runs, or unlock new vehicles. Biomes shift as you travel further — each has unique hazards. Bosses appear every ~1200 score.

Tips

Use checkpoints to heal when low on HP. Save your best drawings as reusable parts. The Veteran loadout gives you 20s before the first part fails — great for long runs.
